How this works

  • Select a date, then use Mark period day to toggle.
  • Predictions use your typical cycle/period lengths + history (±2 day window).
  • Fertile window = ovulation −5…+1; ovulation ≈ next period − luteal length (default 14d).
  • Data stays in your browser (localStorage). Export regularly if needed.
  • Not medical advice; consult a professional for clinical guidance.
