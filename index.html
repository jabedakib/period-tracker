How Luna Lite works

  • Click a calendar day (or use the date picker) then use Mark period day to toggle.
  • Predictions use your typical cycle/period lengths and the history of starts (±2 day window).
  • Fertile window = ovulation −5…+1, with ovulation ≈ next period − luteal length (default 14d).
  • All data stays in your browser (localStorage). Export regularly if needed.
  • This is not medical advice; consult a professional for clinical guidance.
